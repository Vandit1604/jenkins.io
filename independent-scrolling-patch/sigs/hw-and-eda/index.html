Description

This special interest group of Jenkins users and practitioners focuses on Electronic Design Automation with Jenkins, and on any aspects of running CI/CD flows with hardware in the loop.

Focus Areas

  • CI/CD for hardware

  • Usage of EDA Tools in Jenkins

  • ASIC and FPGA RDF flows with Jenkins

  • Hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) testing on Jenkins

  • Jenkins plugins for the area

  • Coordinating development projects related to the area (e.g. Google Summer of Code projects)

Meetings

SIG meetings will be held on a monthly basis. Follow the mailing list and Gitter channel to participate in scheduling.
